About Modern Pong

Pong is known to be one of the first digital games, and it revolutionised the gaming landscape. But that's all old news. This is Modern Pong! The highly anticipated reworked version of the classic. Multiple difficulty's, intricate AI, local multiplayer and of course beautiful visuals.

Features

Beautiful Visuals

Visuals is a core component that the original game was lacking. I gave the game beautiful visuals, sfx and practical effects like screenshake to make the game feel a lot better to play than the original from 1972.

Settings

As this was my first ever Unity project I wanted to make some of the most common features. I made a simple settings menu with a volume slider and a difficulty selector that will change the whole game.

AI

Playing games with friends and family is fun and all but not everyone has them at their disposal. So I made a simple AI that will act as the second player when playing single player.
