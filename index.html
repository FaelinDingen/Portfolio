Hey there! I'm Faelin, a 17-year-old software developer and avid game enthusiast hailing from Noord Brabant, Netherlands.

My journey into the world of programming began with a simple curiosity back in 2018, and I've been hooked ever since. I've dabbled in various digital skills, from programming and 3D modeling in Blender to video and image editing. My main passion will always be front-end software development, especially making games.

Looking ahead, I aspire to craft exceptional games and, who knows, maybe even start my own game development venture one day.

Outside the tech world, you can find me climbing boulders, shooting bows, exploring cool places and playing many types of games. Learning new skills is a personal hobby of mine, and I'm always eager to explore new horizons.

You're welcome to browse through my portfolio, and if you have any questions, don't hesitate to contact me.
